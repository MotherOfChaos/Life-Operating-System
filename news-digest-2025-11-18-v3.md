### Breakthrough: Anxiety Controlled by Immune Cells, Not Neurons

**WHAT HAPPENED:**
- Researchers discovered anxiety controlled by **two dueling groups of immune cells (microglia)** in brain
- Microglia act like biological pedals - one pushing anxiety forward, other holding back
- **Not neuron-based** as traditionally believed

**WHY IT MATTERS:**
Complete paradigm shift. Opens entirely new therapeutic approach - targeting immune system rather than neurotransmitters. Could revolutionize anxiety treatment.
