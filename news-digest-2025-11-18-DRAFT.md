**WHAT HAPPENED:**

- These microglia act like biological pedals‚Äîone pushing anxiety forward, the other holding it back
- Challenges traditional neuron-centric view of mental health

**WHY IT MATTERS:**
Opens entirely new therapeutic approach for anxiety disorders - targeting immune system rather than just neurotransmitters.

### Brain Focus: Rotating Waves Restore Attention

**WHAT HAPPENED:**
- MIT's Picower Institute found **rotating waves of brain activity** help restore focus after distractions
- In animal tests, **full rotations correlated with better performance**
- Sleep deprivation causes attention lapses that coincide with fluid flushing from brain (process that normally occurs during sleep)

**RELEVANCE TO HMI:**
Understanding attention mechanisms critical for human-machine interaction design - when brain is ready to process information vs when it needs recovery.

**WHAT HAPPENED:**

- Shows brain has built-in precision pain control mechanisms

**WHY IT MATTERS:**
Could revolutionize targeted pain treatments - explaining why some pain conditions harder to treat than others.
